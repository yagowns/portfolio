About_

Hello, my name is Yago Murakami, born and raised in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, currently living and working in Lisbon, Portugal. Graduated in Visual Design, with over 5 years of experience in graphic and web design that is accompanied by a set of self-taught web-development skills. I had the privilege to work with brands such as Nike, Gatorade, Nutella, Hendricks, Cointreau, WomenSecret, Syoss and Schwarzkopf.

With an ever-growing desire to create and experiment, learning has an important role in each creation. From concept to execution of an idea, the whole process of creation is, by itself, immensely rewarding mostly because of its path, rather than its final form.
